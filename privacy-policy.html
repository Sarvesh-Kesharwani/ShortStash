ShortStash

Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 5, 2026

ShortStash is a browser extension that lets you save and categorize YouTube Shorts and Instagram Reels. This policy explains what data the extension accesses, how it is used, and your rights over it.

1. Data We Collect

ShortStash only stores data that you explicitly save. This includes:

  • Video URLs of YouTube Shorts and Instagram Reels you save
  • Video titles and channel/creator names detected from the page
  • Thumbnail image URLs
  • Categories you create and assign to saved videos
  • Timestamps of when videos were saved

No data is ever collected passively. The extension only captures information about a video when you actively click the save button.

2. Where Data Is Stored

All data is stored locally in your browser using the Chrome storage.local API. It never leaves your device unless you choose to use the Google Drive backup feature.

If you enable Google Drive backup, your library data is exported as a JSON file and saved directly to your own Google Drive account using the drive.file scope — which limits access strictly to files created by ShortStash itself. No one else, including the developer, can access your Drive or your backup file.

3. Permissions Used

  • storage — Saves your video library and settings locally in the browser.
  • activeTab — Reads the URL and page content of the currently active tab only when you interact with the extension on YouTube or Instagram. No background tab scanning occurs.
  • identity — Used solely for Google OAuth authentication when you choose to connect Google Drive for backup/restore. Authentication is handled entirely by Google.

4. Google Drive Integration

The Google Drive backup feature is entirely optional. When used:

  • Authentication is performed via Google's official OAuth 2.0 flow.
  • Only the drive.file scope is requested, which restricts access to files that ShortStash itself creates — the extension cannot read or modify any other files in your Drive.
  • Your library data is stored as a single JSON file in your Drive. ShortStash never reads, transmits, or processes this file on any server.
  • You can revoke access at any time from your Google Account permissions page.

5. Data Sharing

ShortStash does not share, sell, rent, or transmit your data to any third party. There are no analytics, tracking scripts, or remote servers involved. The extension operates entirely client-side within your browser.

6. Content Scripts

ShortStash injects a content script on YouTube and Instagram pages. This script:

  • Adds a "Save" button to Shorts and Reels pages.
  • Reads the current video's URL, title, and thumbnail only when you click Save.
  • Does not run on any other websites.
  • Does not track your browsing history or collect data in the background.

7. Data Deletion

You are in full control of your data:

  • Delete individual saved videos from the library at any time.
  • Remove all data by uninstalling the extension (browser storage is cleared automatically).
  • Delete your Google Drive backup file directly from your Drive if you used that feature.

8. Children's Privacy

ShortStash is not directed at children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect any information from children.

9. Changes to This Policy

If this privacy policy changes, the updated version will be published here with a revised "Last updated" date. Continued use of the extension after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

10. Contact

If you have questions or concerns about this privacy policy, please open an issue on the project's GitHub repository.
